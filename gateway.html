Gateway Commerce Center

The Gateway Commerce Center capitalizes on the dense infrastructural buildup of the region-with rail, barge, truck, and air facilities all close at hand. From a logistical standpoint, the geographic and infrastructural position of the American Bottom remains a site of nearly messianic centrality--whose success is a sort of geographic fait accompli. And, if current tenants are any indication, the likes of Hershey, Unilever, and Procter & Gamble agree.
